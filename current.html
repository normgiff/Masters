Current Investigations

Web Server Delay
Daniel and Norm will investigate how fast our web server (hosted on a BeagleBone Black) may take to get back to the user, and what happens if the client times out for some reason. Robust testing will be needed!

Parts to Purchase
Daniel and Norm are currently investigating what parts to purchase. The parts they purchase will determine where they need unidirectional voltage translators.

Unidirectional Voltage Levelers
Daniel and Norm are currently investigating where to place unidirectional voltage levelers throughout the system. These devices are needed because, for instance, the DUT may run on 5V while the FPGA runs on 3.3V.

Modulating GND for Schmooing
We have figured out how to modulate VDD for schmooing, but not yet GND. We are leaving this investigation as a stretch goal.
